Dragon of Icespire Peak
"Dragon of Icespire Peak" is an exciting Dungeons & Dragons adventure set in the frontier town of Phandalin. Designed for both new and experienced players, the adventure sends characters on a quest to tackle various challenges, including battling dangerous creatures, exploring treacherous locales, and aiding the townspeople. The story revolves around a white dragon threat, with players uncovering a plot that leads them into a series of interconnected quests. With its flexible structure and engaging quests, "Dragon of Icespire Peak" offers a versatile and accessible tabletop experience, perfect for those seeking a mix of classic fantasy elements and heroic exploration.

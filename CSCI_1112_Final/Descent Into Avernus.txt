Baldur's Gate Decent Into Avernus
"Baldur's Gate: Descent Into Avernus" is a captivating Dungeons & Dragons adventure that takes players on a perilous journey from the city of Baldur's Gate to the hellish landscapes of the Nine Hells. Tasked with saving the city from a diabolical plot, players are thrust into a dark and treacherous realm where they must navigate through layers of infernal bureaucracy, confront fiendish adversaries, and make harrowing decisions to survive and thrive. As they descend deeper into Avernus, players must grapple with moral dilemmas, forge alliances, and unravel the secrets of this infernal domain, creating a thrilling and morally complex tabletop experience that seamlessly blends epic fantasy with infernal horror.

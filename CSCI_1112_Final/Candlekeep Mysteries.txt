Candlekeep Mysteries
"Candlekeep Mysteries" is a compilation of Dungeons & Dragons adventures that invites players and Dungeon Masters to delve into the secrets of the renowned library fortress, Candlekeep. This anthology comprises seventeen unique and self-contained stories, each presenting a captivating mystery or quest that ranges from puzzles and investigations to epic battles and imaginative challenges. Set within the rich lore of the Forgotten Realms, these adventures cater to various playstyles and levels of experience, offering a diverse array of storytelling opportunities and opportunities for creative exploration within the expansive world of D&D.

Waterdeep Dragon Heist
"Waterdeep: Dragon Heist" is an engaging Dungeons & Dragons adventure set in the bustling city of Waterdeep, where players embark on a high-stakes treasure hunt. Tasked with finding a hidden cache of gold, players navigate a complex web of intrigue, political maneuvering, and urban adventure. As they uncover clues and navigate through various factions and colorful characters, they must make critical choices that could shape the fate of the city. With its emphasis on urban exploration, heists, and uncovering secrets, "Dragon Heist" offers players a dynamic and immersive experience that blends mystery, role-playing, and thrilling encounters in the heart of one of the Forgotten Realms' most iconic cities.

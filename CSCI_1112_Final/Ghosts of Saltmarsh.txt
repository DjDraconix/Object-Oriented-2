Ghostsof Saltmarsh
"Ghosts of Saltmarsh" is a versatile Dungeons & Dragons adventure that allows players to embark on nautical and coastal quests set in the town of Saltmarsh and its surrounding waters. This anthology-style adventure combines seven different adventures, all of which can be played individually or woven into a larger campaign. From uncovering haunted mysteries and battling sea creatures to navigating political intrigues and piracy, the adventures provide a diverse range of challenges and opportunities for both land-based and maritime gameplay. With its maritime focus, "Ghosts of Saltmarsh" offers players a chance to explore high seas, uncover secrets, and engage in thrilling swashbuckling action, making it a must-play for those seeking a maritime twist on classic D&D storytelling.

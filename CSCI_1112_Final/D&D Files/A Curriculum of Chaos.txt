Strixhaven A Curriculum of Chaos
"Strixhaven: A Curriculum of Chaos" is a Magic: The Gathering expansion set that immerses players into the magical world of Strixhaven University. The set features five distinct colleges, each representing different magical disciplines, and showcases the clash of ideologies and the pursuit of knowledge among the students. As part of the Strixhaven series, "A Curriculum of Chaos" introduces new cards, mechanics, and lore, highlighting the ongoing struggles and rivalries within the university. Players can explore the complexities of these colleges, their unique spells, and characters as they engage in exciting battles and strategic gameplay within the vibrant and enchanting realm of Strixhaven.

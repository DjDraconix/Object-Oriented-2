Lost Mines of Phandelver
"Lost Mines of Phandelver" is an introductory Dungeons & Dragons adventure designed for new players and Dungeon Masters. Set in the frontier town of Phandalin, the adventure tasks players with investigating the disappearance of a dwarf named Gundren Rockseeker and his valuable cargo. As players follow the trail, they uncover a network of goblin and bandit threats, leading them through dungeons, encounters with dangerous creatures, and choices that can shape the course of the story. With its straightforward narrative, diverse challenges, and opportunities for role-playing, "Lost Mines of Phandelver" provides an accessible and engaging entry point into the world of tabletop role-playing games.
